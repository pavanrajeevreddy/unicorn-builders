Company Introduction

UNICORN BUILDERS is a modern construction and real estate development company established in 2024. The company was founded by SANAMPUDI JANAKIRAM REDDY along with his wife SANAMPUDI AUMULYA. Their vision is to deliver high-quality residential apartments that combine modern architecture, durability, and comfortable living spaces for families.

Since its establishment, the company has rapidly gained trust in the real estate market by focusing on quality construction, transparent dealings, and timely project delivery. UNICORN BUILDERS has successfully completed multiple residential projects and continues to expand with new developments and pre-booking opportunities for upcoming apartments.
